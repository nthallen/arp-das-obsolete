%C [options] <stepfilename> <spsfilename>
Step to Snafu Spreadsheet filter. Only 1000 series and 2010,2110 step formats
supported.
	-a	don't ask about creating spreadsheets for auxillary vars
	-g	don't assume time is GMT
	-m	independent variable is not monotonically increasing
	-i <independent var>	which var to use; 1,2,...; format 2010.
